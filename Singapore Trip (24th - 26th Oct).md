
Here’s a suggested plan for your short weekend getaway to Singapore, focused on visiting the Harry Potter: Visions of Magic exhibition at Resorts World Sentosa.

---

**Trip Overview**

- **Dates:** 24th – 26th October 2025
    
- **Travelers:** You, your brother, his wife, and their two kids
    
- **Destination:** Singapore (main event: Harry Potter Visions of Magic at Resorts World Sentosa)
    

---

**Day 1: Friday, 24th October**

- **Morning:**
    
    - Take an early flight from Kuala Lumpur to Singapore (Changi Airport). Aim for a flight that arrives by mid-morning to maximize your day.
        
    - After arrival, clear immigration and take a taxi or Grab to your hotel (preferably near Sentosa or HarbourFront for convenience).
        
- **Afternoon:**
    
    - Check in, freshen up, and have lunch nearby.
        
    - Optional: Explore Sentosa Island’s other attractions (e.g., S.E.A. Aquarium, Adventure Cove Waterpark) or take it easy at Siloso Beach.
        
- **Evening:**
    
    - Have dinner at one of the many family-friendly restaurants on Sentosa or VivoCity mall.
        
    - Rest early to be fresh for your main event the next day.
        

---

**Day 2: Saturday, 25th October**

- **Morning to Afternoon:**
    
    - Breakfast at the hotel.
        
    - Head to Resorts World Sentosa for the Harry Potter: Visions of Magic experience. Book your tickets in advance!
        
    - Allow 2–3 hours for the exhibition (kids will love the interactive displays and photo ops).
        
- **Lunch:**
    
    - Enjoy lunch at Resorts World Sentosa—many options for families.
        
- **Afternoon:**
    
    - Explore more of Sentosa or Universal Studios Singapore if time and budget allow.
        
    - Alternatively, visit the Trick Eye Museum or Madame Tussauds.
        
- **Evening:**
    
    - Dinner at Sentosa or head back to the city for a night view at Marina Bay.
        
    - Optional: Gardens by the Bay light show (kids will love it).
        

---

**Day 3: Sunday, 26th October**

- **Morning:**

    - Leisurely breakfast and check out.
    - If your flight is later, visit Orchard Road for some shopping or explore Chinatown/Little India for a cultural experience.

- **Afternoon:**
    
    - Head to Changi Airport for your flight back to Kuala Lumpur.


---

**Travel Tips:**

- Book flights and Harry Potter exhibition tickets early.
- Consider family rooms or connecting rooms at hotels for convenience.
- Use public transport or Grab for easy movement around the city.
- Bring travel adaptors and some SGD cash for small purchases.

Here’s a budget comparison for your Singapore weekend getaway, with two scenarios: (A) staying in Singapore and (B) staying in Johor Bahru and commuting to Singapore. All prices are in MYR and are estimated for 4 adults + 2 kids, for 2 nights (24–26 October 2025).

---

**A. Staying in Singapore**

1. **Flights (KUL–SIN round-trip, economy, 6 pax):**  
    ~RM 350 per person × 6 = **RM 2,100**
    
2. **Accommodation (budget hotel/family room):**  
    Average RM 700/night × 2 nights = **RM 1,400**
    
3. **Transport (airport transfers, local taxi/Grab/MRT):**  
    ~RM 250 total
    
4. **Harry Potter Visions of Magic tickets:**  
    Estimated RM 150 per adult × 4 = RM 600  
    Estimated RM 100 per child × 2 = RM 200  
    **Total = RM 800**
    
5. **Food (hawker centres, cafes, simple restaurants):**  
    ~RM 40 per person per day × 6 × 2 days = **RM 480**
    
6. **Other attractions/activities:**  
    Optional, RM 100 per person × 6 = **RM 600**
    
7. **Miscellaneous (souvenirs, snacks, etc.):**  
    ~RM 200
    

**Total (Staying in Singapore):**  
**RM 2,100 (flights) + RM 1,400 (hotel) + RM 250 (transport) + RM 800 (tickets) + RM 480 (food) + RM 600 (activities) + RM 200 (misc.) = RM 5,830**

---

**B. Staying in Johor Bahru**

1. **Flights (KUL–JHB round-trip, 6 pax):**  
    ~RM 250 per person × 6 = **RM 1,500**
    
2. **Accommodation (Johor Bahru hotel/family room):**  
    Average RM 300/night × 2 nights = **RM 600**
    
3. **Transport (JB–Singapore daily, return, private car/van or bus):**  
    Private car/van: ~RM 200/day × 2 = RM 400  
    Bus: ~RM 20 per person × 6 × 2 days = RM 240  
    (Estimate using private van for comfort: **RM 400**)
    
4. **Harry Potter Visions of Magic tickets:**  
    Same as above = **RM 800**
    
5. **Food (mix of JB and SG meals):**  
    ~RM 30 per person per day × 6 × 2 days = **RM 360**
    
6. **Other attractions/activities:**  
    Optional, RM 80 per person × 6 = **RM 480**
    
7. **Miscellaneous (souvenirs, snacks, etc.):**  
    ~RM 200
    

**Total (Staying in Johor Bahru):**  
**RM 1,500 (flights) + RM 600 (hotel) + RM 400 (transport) + RM 800 (tickets) + RM 360 (food) + RM 480 (activities) + RM 200 (misc.) = RM 4,340**
